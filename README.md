# Reproducibility Repository for the PhD Thesis of Janette Suherli (2026)

This repository serves as an archival reproducibility resource accompanying the PhD thesis and associated publications of Janette Suherli (2026). It contains analysis workflows, figure-generation and visualization scripts, modelling utilities, and supplementary tools used throughout the thesis work.

The thesis, titled

> **Probing the Environments of Central Compact Objects in Supernova Remnants with Optical Integral Field Spectroscopy**

focuses on the study of central compact object (CCO) environments in young supernova remnants (SNRs) using optical integral field spectroscopy (IFS), primarily based on observations obtained with the Multi Unit Spectroscopic Explorer (MUSE) on the ESO Very Large Telescope (VLT).

The repository includes materials associated with three major studies:
1. the characterization of the optical nebula surrounding the Vela Jr CCO (Suherli, J., Safi-Harb, S., Seitenzahl, I. R., et al., 2024, MNRAS, 527, 9263),
2. the revised distance determination to the Vela Jr SNR through its association with the Ve 7–27 (HH 1270) Herbig–Haro system (Suherli, J., Seitenzahl, I. R., Safi-Harb, S., et al., 2026, ApJL, 997, L36), and
3. the compact object environment in the oxygen-rich SNR 1E 0102.2–7219 (E0102; Suherli, J., Safi-Harb, S., Seitenzahl, I. R., et al., 2026, submitted to ApJ).
